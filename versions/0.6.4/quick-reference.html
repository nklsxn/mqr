Reliability#

See the comprehensive reliability library for:

  • parametric and non-parametric lifetime modelling,

  • accelerated life testing,

  • repairable systems,

  • reliability testing,

  • models of physical failure: stress-cycles, stress-strain, stress-life, creep, acceleration factors, etc., and

  • censored lifetime testing data.

MQR and reliability can be used together, and improvement efforts can just as easily target product reliability as product quality.
